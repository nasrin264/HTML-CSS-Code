Primeasia University

Primeasia university is a place of excellence in educating Boys and Girls, and in meeting the demand of the country. Primeasia University is one of the most reputed private universities at Dhaka in Bangladesh. Primeasia university has superb laboratory facilities in different department and superb library facilities. Primeasia University has highly qualified teaching faculties with have higher degrees in their disciplines. Textile department, one of the leading departments of Primeasia University is well connected with leading garments and textile factories and related business organizations.
